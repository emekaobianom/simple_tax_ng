Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SimpleTaxNigeria Calculator handles user information. Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to being transparent and responsible.

App Information

App Name: SimpleTaxNigeria Calculator
Platform: Android (Google Play Store)
Purpose: To help users understand and simplify Nigerian tax calculations.

Data Collection

SimpleTaxNigeria Calculator does NOT collect any personal or sensitive user data.

  • No name, email address, phone number, or identity information is collected.
  • No financial or payment information is collected.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No device identifiers are collected.

Offline Usage

This app is a fully offline application. All calculations are performed directly on your device.

  • No internet connection is required.
  • No data is sent to any server.
  • No data is stored remotely.

User Accounts & Login

SimpleTaxNigeria Calculator does not require user registration or login.

  • No accounts are created.
  • No authentication is required.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services such as:

  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Tracking SDKs

Children’s Privacy

SimpleTaxNigeria Calculator does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect any data at all, it is safe for general use.

Security

Because the app does not collect, store, or transmit any user data, there is no risk of personal data exposure through this application.
